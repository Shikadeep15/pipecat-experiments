How it works
WebRTC Echo Cancellation: Your browser's built-in echo cancellation filters out the bot's voice, so you don't need headphones!

SmartTurn (Pipecat-style): The bot uses intelligent turn detection that analyzes your speech patterns. It waits for complete thoughts, not just silence pauses. Try saying "I want to order... hmm... maybe a pizza" - it will wait for you to finish!

Pipeline: Your voice -> Deepgram STT -> SmartTurn -> GPT-4o-mini -> ElevenLabs (Neha) -> Audio

Plivo Phone: This bot can also receive phone calls via Plivo. Configure your Plivo webhook to point to /plivo/answer
